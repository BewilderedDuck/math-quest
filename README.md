# Math Quest

A simple maths study tool like Duolingo, focusing on GCD and LCM Euler content for Year 7-9 students.

## About

Math Quest is an interactive learning platform designed to help students in Years 7-9 master fundamental mathematical concepts. The app focuses on:

- **GCD (Greatest Common Divisor)** - Finding the largest number that divides two or more numbers
- **LCM (Least Common Multiple)** - Finding the smallest number that is a multiple of two or more numbers
- **Prime Factorisation** - The process of breaking down a number into the prime numbers that multiply together to equal the original number

With a Duolingo-style interface, Math Quest makes learning these Euler-related concepts engaging and fun.

## Tech Stack
